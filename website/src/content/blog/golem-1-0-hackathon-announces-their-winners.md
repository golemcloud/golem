The Golem 1.0 Hackathon happened this past August 31st, 2024 and was a total success. Celebrating [the launch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XEFPLz18ADA&ab_channel=DurableComputing) of Golem 1.0 contestants from all over the world came together to build solutions to the Hackathon problem on Golem.

Watch the full Hackathon here:

<iframe allowfullscreen="true" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/z25QbhTjpXI"></iframe>

After each contestant presented their submissions, the Golem Team awarded 1st place to Daniele Torelli, second to Glenn Lewis, and third to Seeta Ramayya.
