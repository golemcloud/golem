Last week, we delved into the flexibility of Golem Cloud, where we migrated a module of our auction app to Python. We showcased Golem's ability to work seamlessly with different programming languages and demonstrated how Golem Cloud interacts with a language familiar to many developers.

This week, we are continuing our journey through the programming languages supported by **Golem Cloud by writing a part of our auction app in Go**. Golem is currently fully compatible with Rust, Go, C/C++, and Python, and more are coming! You can learn more from the [technical documentation](https://www.golem.cloud/learn).
