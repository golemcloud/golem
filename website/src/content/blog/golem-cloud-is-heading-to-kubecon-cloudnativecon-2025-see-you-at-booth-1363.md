We're thrilled to announce that **Golem Cloud** will be at **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon 2025**, the premier event for developers, architects, and technologists who are shaping the future of cloud-native computing.

📍 **Find us at Booth #1363**

At Golem Cloud, our mission is to make **agent-native applications** resilient, scalable, and developer-friendly. We've built a platform that lets teams run distributed, stateful applications that automatically recover from crashes, handle failures gracefully, and guarantee exactly-once execution — all without complex infrastructure management.

Whether you're building AI agents, event-driven systems, or distributed workflows, Golem Cloud makes it easy to:

- Keep application state consistent across failures
- Avoid duplicate executions and manual retries
- Gain full visibility into execution history
- Scale effortlessly across Kubernetes environments

### What You'll See at Our Booth

Stop by Booth #1363 to meet the team and see live demos of:

- **Stateful Agent Orchestration:** How agents can survive restarts and resume seamlessly.
- **Durable Compute for Cloud-Native Apps:** Running at scale with full reliability.
- **Developer Simplicity:** Using your favorite languages (TypeScript, Rust, and more).

We'll also be sharing insights into where the future of agent-based and event-driven architectures is heading — and how Golem Cloud can help you get there faster.

### Let's Connect

We'd love to chat with you! Swing by Booth #1363 to see a demo, grab some swag, and talk about distributed computing, WebAssembly, and the next generation of cloud-native development.
