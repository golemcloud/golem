Exciting developments are happening over at Golem, and we want to bring you up to speed! We're organizing an online event centered around Golem's API Gateway and the Golem Management Console on **October 6th, 2023, at 11:00 AM (PT)**.

We have developed an interactive user interface that lets us deploy and manage Golem workers with ease. During this event, we will discuss how to use the **Management Console**, and what it has to offer.

Furthermore, we'll delve into the **API Gateway**. It allows defining HTTP routes and helps you decide how they're managed by Golem workers, opening the door to building full-stack applications with Golem.

We're thrilled to have **John A. De Goes**, **Adam Fraser**, and **Nico Burniske**, who will share their insights and experiences in building on Golem Cloud. We will also have a special slot with **Terry Lin** (who is the first user of Golem Developer Preview), discussing his experience with Golem and the potential of the new features!

Join us, connect with the Community, and discover the latest in durable computing.
