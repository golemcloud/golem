## **About Golem**

Golem is an open-source AI agent compute platform that automatically persists and replays agent operations for seamless fault tolerance, letting you build reliable, stateful AI agents without any infrastructure headaches.

## **Hackathon Overview**

Golem is returning to LambdaConf with another hackathon, and this is one hackathon you won't want to miss! Core contributors to Golem are coming on-site to guide you through the process of building your first application on Golem, in your chosen language (Go, Python, TypeScript, JavaScript, Rust, C/C++, MoonBit).

With a chance to win more than $5k in prizes, as well as learn how to build reliable, elastic agents and workflows, the Golem hackathon will develop your skills, expand your network, bolster your resume, and potentially result in accolades for 3 lucky winners!

## **Prizes**

- **1st Place**: $3,000
- **2nd Place**: $1,500
- **3rd Place**: $750

## **Theme & Judging Criteria**

The hackathon will feature a surprise theme to be announced during the morning kickoff. This approach ensures a level playing field for all participants, as no pre-work can be done before the event.

Projects will be evaluated based on:

- How well they satisfy the theme and goals (announced morning of the event)
- Fit and finish of the final product
- Handling of edge cases
- Overall architecture

## **Schedule**

- **9:00 AM**: Introductory remarks, hackathon parameters, and theme announcement
- **9:30 AM**: The hacking begins
- **12:00 Noon**: Working lunch
- **5:00 PM**: Demos & judging
- **6:00 PM**: Winners announced
